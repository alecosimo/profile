NSGA with acceleration constraints

In this work, the nonsmooth generalized-α time integration is extended to problem involving constraints at acceleration level. Also, the convenience of using constraints at acceleration level for certain kind of problems is studied.

Test case: a sphere rolling over a fixed sphere with zero gravity. They are linked by a preloaded spring.

Result obtain using the decoupled version of the NSGA with constraints at position and velocity levels: the outer sphere artificially accelerates

As it is shown in the following comparison, when that problem is solved taking into account constraints at acceleration level, the outer ball does not accelerates.
