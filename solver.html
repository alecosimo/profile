A robust nonsmooth generalized-α scheme

A robust and accurate time integrator for the dynamic simulation of multibody systems composed by rigid and/or flexible bodies subject to contacts and impacts is proposed. For this purpose, a decoupled version of the original non-smooth generalized-α (NSGA) method is proposed leading to obtain a scheme which is more robust for problems involving flexible elements and non-linear bilateral constraints. In what follows, the results obtained with the original NSGA are denoted by CS (Coupled-Solution), whilst the ones obtained with the proposed extension are denoted by DS (Decoupled-Solution).
