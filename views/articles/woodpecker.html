The study of the woodpecker toy dynamics sets up a good example to show the capabilities of the nonsmooth generalized-α scheme within the context of a general finite element framework. This example has already been studied by many authors who generally adopt a model with a minimal set of coordinates and small rotations. Here, the woodpecker toy is simulated adopting a finite element approach, where the equations of motion are assembled automatically, and large rotations can be easily considered without.
