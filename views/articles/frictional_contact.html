In Odin, the simulation of the non-smooth dynamics of systems composed of rigid and flexible bodies connected by kinematic joints can be done using a nonsmooth monolithic Newton scheme based on an augmented Lagrangian. In addition, Odin has been interfaced to the So-Bogus library. Which offers the possibility to adopt Gauss-Seidel like iterative solvers for the solution of frictional contact problems (for the moment without considering bilateral constraints). The equations of motion are integrated using the nonsmooth generalized-α time integration scheme proposed by our group.
