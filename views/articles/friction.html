A new frictional contact element for the simulation of the non-smooth dynamics of systems composed of rigid and flexible bodies connected by kinematic joints is proposed. The equations of motion are integrated using a nonsmooth generalized-α time integration scheme and the frictional contact problem is formulated using a mixed approach, based on an augmented Lagrangian technique and a Coulomb friction law.
